Exam Subjects Time Duration Marks Type

Paper-1 Essay 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-2 General Studies-I 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-3 General Studies-II 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-4 General Studies-III 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-5 General Studies-IV 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-B Optioanl-I 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits
Paper-B Optioanl-II 3 Hour 250 Marks Merits

IAS Prelims Syllabus-2021

    General Studies-1

  • Current Events
  • History of India and Indian National Movement.
  • Indian and World Geograohy
  • Indian Polity and Governance
  • Economics and Social Development
  • Environmental and Ecology Biodiversity and Climate Change
  • General Science

    General Studies-2 (CSAT)-2021

  • Comprehension
  • Interpersonal Skill Including Communication Skill
  • Logical Reasoning and Analytical Ability
  • Decision-Making and Problem Solving
  • General Mental Ability
  • Basic Numeracy (Class X Level)
  • Data Interpretation (Class X Level)

    Mains Syllabus-2021

  • Paper-A Compulsory Any One Indian Language
  • English Compulsory
  • Paper-1 Essay
  • Paper-2 General Studies-1 Indian Heritage & Culture..
  • Paper-3 General Studies-2 Governance, Constitution..
  • Paper-4 General Studies-3 Economic Dovelopments..
  • Optional Paper-1
  • Optional Paper-2
